Our Heritage & Craft

The Journey of Suman Poly Silk

Home / About Us
ESTABLISHED 1998

Milestones of Luxury Manufacturing

1998

Fledgling Beginnings

Founded as a small silk yarn processing plant with just 10 traditional looms on the outskirts of Surat, Gujarat.

2006

Industrial Modernization

Implemented automated high-speed B2B power looms, expanding the manufacturing catalog into bridal silk wear.

2015

Global Export Enterprise

Inaugurated international trade operations, supplying wholesale fashion lines across Southeast Asia, North America, and the United Kingdom.

2026

Next-Gen Digital Printing Labs

Opened state-of-the-art sustainable manufacturing units, combining eco-safe dye baths with advanced digital pattern engines.

Our Vision

To redefine the global premium saree market by merging centuries-old Indian weaving artistry with sustainable, zero-defect modern manufacturing processes.

Our Mission

To provide boutique designers and wholesale retailers globally with luxurious fabrics, design excellence, reliable logistics, and high-volume order consistency.

FACTORY CORNERSTONE

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

Our flagship factory spans over 100,000 square feet, operating round-the-clock under strict quality control. Equipped with Swiss jacquard loom electronics and computerized yarn testing benches, we eliminate visual inconsistencies from our silk sheets.

We prioritize sustainable dyes and water recyclability systems, ensuring our luxury manufacturing processes remain gentle on environmental resources.
